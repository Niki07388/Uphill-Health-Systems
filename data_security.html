Data Security & Privacy Policy

At Uphill Health, your privacy and the security of your health information are our top priorities. We are committed to protecting your data with robust security measures and strict privacy practices.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We understand the sensitive nature of health information. Our privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal and health data. We adhere to industry best practices and applicable regulations (e.g., HIPAA compliance principles) to ensure your information remains confidential.

We will never sell or share your personal health information with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Data Encryption

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols. This ensures that your information is protected from unauthorized access during transmission.

Your health records and personal data stored on our servers are also encrypted at rest, adding another layer of security against breaches.

Access Control

Access to your personal health information is strictly controlled and limited to authorized personnel who require it to provide you with services. All access is logged and regularly audited.

Our internal systems and processes are designed to minimize the risk of unauthorized access or misuse of your data.

Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Once data is no longer needed, it is securely disposed of.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal health information.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Withdraw consent for certain data processing activities.

For any privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@uphillhealth.com.
