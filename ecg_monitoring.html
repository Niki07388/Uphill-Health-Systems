ECG Monitoring

Keep track of your heart health with our integrated ECG monitoring solution. Get real-time data, trend analysis, and alerts for any irregularities.

Real-time Data

Monitor your heart's electrical activity as it happens. View live ECG waveforms and key metrics directly on your dashboard.

Irregularity Alerts

Our system analyzes your ECG data for common arrhythmias and notifies you and your designated healthcare provider of any significant findings.

Historical Reports

Access a comprehensive history of your ECG readings, allowing you and your doctor to track trends and evaluate long-term heart health.

How to get started with ECG Monitoring:

  1. Enroll: Contact our support team to learn about compatible ECG devices and enrollment options.
  2. Connect Device: Once enrolled, connect your personal ECG device (e.g., wearable, patch) to our platform.
  3. Monitor: Begin monitoring your ECG data. Data will be securely transmitted to your Uphill Health account.
  4. Review: View your data on the dashboard and share reports with your healthcare professional.
